Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk FRS (born June 28, 1971) is a South African-Canadian-American engineer, industrial designer, technology entrepreneur and philanthropist. He is the founder, CEO, CTO and chief designer of SpaceX;early investor, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc.;founder of The Boring Company; co-founder of Neuralink; and co-founder and initial co-chairman of OpenAI. He was elected a Fellow of the Royal Society (FRS) in 2018.In December 2016, he was ranked 21st on the Forbes list of The World's Most Powerful People, and was ranked joint-first on the Forbes list of the Most Innovative Leaders of 2019. As of July 14, 2020 his net worth was estimated at $69.4 billion and he is listed by Forbes as the 8th-richest person in the world. He is the longest tenured CEO of any automotive manufacturer globally.
